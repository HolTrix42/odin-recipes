Best Hummus Ever

Ingredients

  • 2 can chickpeas (15 oz)
  • 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 spoon well-stirred tahini
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sweet paprika
  • 2 tablespoons curry massala
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger pownder
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 4 drops tabasco
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt to taste
